Food Insecurity & Diabetes Risk Analysis

I used R, tidyverse, and ggplot2 to analyze over 100,000 CDC NHANES responses on youth aged 12–19, examining links between food insecurity and diabetes risk through two-sample proportion tests with 95% confidence intervals and reproducible, publication-quality visualizations in R Markdown demonstrating full data analysis and clear communication of results.
