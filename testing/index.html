Evans Scholarships

Founded in 1899, the Western Golf Association/Evans Scholars Foundation is a nonprofit that pairs world-class golf—hosting the PGA TOUR’s BMW Championship and four premier amateur events—with Chick Evans’s 1930-founded scholarship program, which provides full tuition and housing awards averaging $120 K to high-achieving caddies. Today it supports over 1,070 scholars at 21 campuses, counts more than 11,550 alumni, and underwrites $25 M in annual awards funded by 33,000+ Par Club members, all backed by $116.5 M in net assets—an enduring legacy of transforming lives through education.
